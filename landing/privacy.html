Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 26, 2026

Information We Collect

When you use StyleMCP, we collect information you provide directly:

  • Account information (email, name) when you sign up
  • Content you submit for brand validation
  • Style packs and brand rules you create
  • API usage data and preferences

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our brand validation services
  • Process your content through our validation and rewrite APIs
  • Communicate with you about your account and usage
  • Ensure the security and reliability of our platform

Data Processing

Content submitted to StyleMCP for validation or rewriting is processed in real-time and is not stored after the request completes, unless you explicitly save it to your account. We do not use your content to train AI models.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. All API communications are encrypted via TLS. Your style packs and brand rules are stored securely and are only accessible to you.

Open Source

The core StyleMCP validation engine is open source under the MIT license. You can review the code, self-host, or contribute at github.com/3DUNLMTD/stylemcp.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us at hello@stylemcp.com
